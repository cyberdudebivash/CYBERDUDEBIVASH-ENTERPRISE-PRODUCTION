The **NIS2 Directive** forces critical infrastructure operators to establish robust cyber risk management frameworks. Compliance requires strict adherence to tight incident reporting timelines. Organizations must submit a **24-hour early warning** and a **72-hour notification** during active breaches. Traditional Security Operations Centers cannot scale to meet these constraints. Deploying an **Autonomous AI SOC** is no longer optional; it is a regulatory necessity.

### The NIS2 Reporting Timeline Bottleneck
Under Article 21, manual alert analysis fails. Analysts cannot triage, contain, and report incidents within 24 hours. The operational gap requires automated orchestration. 

*   **24-Hour Early Warning**: Automated indicators correlation.
*   **72-Hour Detailed Notification**: Deep mitigation overview.

### Architectural Pillars of AI Compliance

*   **Real-Time Alert Triage**: Model pipelines ingest raw telemetry logs.
*   **Proactive AI Threat Hunting**: Continuous incident sweeps across endpoints.
*   **Automated Incident Response**: Triggering Sigma/YARA mitigation rules instantly.
