Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial available? ▼

Yes — the Professional plan includes a full 14-day free trial with no credit card required. You get access to all Professional-tier features from day one. If you don't love it, simply close your account before the trial ends and you won't be charged.

Can I cancel anytime? ▼

Absolutely. Monthly plans can be cancelled at any time with no penalty — your access continues until the end of the current billing period. Annual plans require 30 days notice. We don't believe in locking customers into contracts they don't want.

What payment methods do you accept? ▼

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), wire transfers/SWIFT, and UPI/NEFT for Indian customers. Enterprise and Government customers can also pay via purchase order with Net 30/60 terms. Cryptocurrency payments are available on request.

Do you offer refunds? ▼

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on Starter and Professional plans. If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days of a paid subscription, contact us for a full refund — no questions asked. Enterprise and custom engagements follow the refund terms outlined in your signed contract.

How does the annual billing discount work? ▼

Switching to annual billing saves you 20% across all plans — equivalent to approximately 2.5 months free per year. The full annual amount is invoiced upfront as a single payment. You can switch between monthly and annual cycles at your next renewal date.

Can I upgrade or downgrade mid-cycle? ▼

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately — you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing period. Downgrades take effect at the next renewal date so you retain current-tier features until then. Enterprise plan changes are managed by your dedicated account manager.
