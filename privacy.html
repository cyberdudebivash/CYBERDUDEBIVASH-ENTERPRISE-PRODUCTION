📊 2. Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary ("data minimization" principle) to deliver our services:

Account & Identity Data

  • Full name, business email address, company name
  • Job title, department, country/region
  • Authentication credentials (hashed, never stored in plaintext)
  • API keys (hashed with SHA-256)

Usage & Technical Data

  • IP address (anonymized after 30 days), browser type, OS
  • Pages visited, time spent, features used
  • API call logs (indicator types, not content of lookups)
  • Error logs and performance metrics

Threat Intelligence Data

  • IOCs, IP addresses, domains, hashes you submit for analysis
  • This data is processed ephemerally and not stored beyond your session unless you opt in to history

Payment Data

  • We do not store card numbers. Payments processed via Razorpay/Stripe (PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant)
  • We retain transaction IDs, amounts, and timestamps for accounting compliance

⚙️ 3. How We Use Your Data

Data is used exclusively for:

  • Service Delivery — Providing platform access, API responses, threat intelligence, SOC alerts
  • Security Operations — Detecting abuse, preventing unauthorized access, fraud prevention
  • Product Improvement — Aggregated, anonymized usage analytics to improve features
  • Communication — Service notifications, security alerts, invoices (with explicit consent for marketing)
  • Legal Compliance — Responding to valid legal requests, regulatory reporting

We never use your data for: Selling to third parties, advertising networks, data brokers, training public AI models without explicit consent, or any purpose not stated above.

🤝 4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data. We share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers — AWS (cloud infrastructure), Cloudflare (CDN/DDoS), Razorpay/Stripe (payments), Google Analytics (anonymized). All are bound by DPAs.
  • Legal Requirements — When compelled by valid court order, law enforcement request under Indian IT Act 2000 or GDPR Article 6(1)(c). We will notify you if legally permitted.
  • Business Transfers — In merger/acquisition, data transfers subject to continued protection under this Policy.
  • With Your Consent — Any other sharing requires explicit, informed consent.

🔒 5. Data Security

We implement enterprise-grade security controls aligned with ISO 27001 and SOC 2 Type II:

  • AES-256 encryption at rest; TLS 1.3 in transit
  • Customer-Managed Keys (CMK) available for Enterprise plans
  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) enforced on all admin access
  • Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) with principle of least privilege
  • Annual third-party penetration testing and red team exercises
  • 24/7 SOC monitoring of our own infrastructure
  • Air-gapped backups with 4-hour RTO

In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users within 72 hours as required by GDPR Article 33 and DPDP Act provisions.

🗓️ 6. Data Retention

  • Account data — Retained while account is active + 90 days post-deletion request
  • API logs — 30 days rolling window, then anonymized aggregates
  • Payment records — 7 years (statutory requirement under Companies Act 2013)
  • Security logs — 12 months for incident investigation purposes
  • IOC submissions — Ephemeral by default; 30 days if history enabled

⚖️ 7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access — Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification — Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure — Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restriction — Limit processing of your data
  • Right to Portability — Receive your data in a machine-readable format (JSON/CSV)
  • Right to Object — Object to processing for marketing or profiling
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — At any time, without affecting prior processing

To exercise any right, email privacy@cyberdudebivash.com. We will respond within 30 days.

🍪 8. Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal, essential cookies only:

  • Essential Cookies — Session management, authentication, CSRF protection (cannot be disabled)
  • Analytics — Google Analytics with IP anonymization enabled. You can opt out via browser settings or our cookie banner
  • No advertising cookies — We do not run retargeting, Facebook Pixel, or any advertising trackers

Manage cookie preferences at any time via the cookie settings icon in the bottom-left corner of any page.

🔗 9. Third-Party Services

Our platforms integrate with industry-standard services. Each is bound by a Data Processing Agreement (DPA):

  • Amazon Web Services — Primary cloud infrastructure (AP-South-1, Mumbai)
  • Cloudflare — DDoS protection, WAF, global CDN
  • Razorpay / Stripe — Payment processing (PCI DSS Level 1)
  • Google Analytics — Anonymized web analytics
  • SendGrid — Transactional email delivery

👶 10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us data, contact privacy@cyberdudebivash.com and we will delete it immediately.

🌍 11. International Data Transfers

By default, data is stored in Mumbai, India (AWS AP-South-1). Enterprise customers can select:

  • 🇮🇳 India — AWS Mumbai (default)
  • 🇪🇺 European Union — AWS Frankfurt (GDPR-native)
