⬡ Who We Are

Built by Security Researchers, for the Enterprise

CYBERDUDEBIVASH® was founded in 2020 by Bivash — an elite ethical hacker, penetration tester, and threat intelligence researcher with deep expertise across offensive security, malware analysis, and AI-driven defense systems. What started as a personal cybersecurity blog and open-source tools repository has evolved into a globally recognized enterprise cybersecurity platform trusted by organizations across six continents.

Today, CYBERDUDEBIVASH® operates a full-spectrum security ecosystem comprising five flagship platforms: Sentinel APEX™ (real-time threat intelligence), AI Security Hub (autonomous AI-driven analysis), ThreatCore™ Tools (150+ open and enterprise security instruments), the Intelligence API (machine-readable threat data feeds), and the Research Blog (original vulnerability research and threat advisories). Our platforms process over 2 million security events per day, correlating indicators of compromise across darknet forums, malware repositories, CVE databases, and live honeypot networks.

We combine the agility of a boutique security research firm with the infrastructure capabilities of an enterprise vendor. Our team of offensive security engineers, AI researchers, and SOC analysts work around the clock to deliver proactive, intelligence-led cybersecurity — not reactive checkbox compliance. We believe security should be a strategic business enabler, not a cost center.

⬡ Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

🎯
Our Mission

To democratize enterprise-grade cybersecurity by providing every organization — from startups to the largest enterprises — with access to cutting-edge threat intelligence, AI-powered defense tools, and world-class security research. We exist to make the digital world measurably safer by closing the information asymmetry between threat actors and defenders.

We achieve this by publishing free tools, open-source intelligence frameworks, and actionable vulnerability research while delivering bespoke enterprise security engagements that drive measurable risk reduction across every sector.

🔭
Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted AI-native cybersecurity intelligence platform — a single unified ecosystem where threat actors are identified, tracked, and neutralized before they cause harm. We envision a future where autonomous AI defenders proactively eliminate threats in milliseconds, where every organization has a virtual CISO-grade security posture.

By 2030, we aim to protect over 10,000 organizations globally with zero-trust, AI-first security architectures powered by CYBERDUDEBIVASH® intelligence, and to establish India as the global cybersecurity innovation frontier.
